WE ARE REDSHIFT

Student-driven startup out of Cluj-Napoca, Romania. We fully intend to bring the newest talent to the international playing field - starting today.

OUR PAST

Initially concieved as a student team for the European CanSat competitions in 2023, we gradually took on more and more talented individuals until we grew into what we are today. Restless. And ready.

OUR FUTURE

Incredibly volatile. Yet the potential is infinite. Our aspirations reach far - but we have yet to see the edges of our limitations. And until we hit a ceiling, we'll keep going. And after we do - we'll break through it.

CANSAT
SATELLITE DEVELOPMENT AND CONSTRUCTION

The birth of our team, in 2023, revisited in 2024. Throughout, we built the foundations that would go on to represent the net sum of RedShift Aerospace today. Out of 8 teams, RedShift Aerospace was able to secure the fourth in 2023, and second in 2024 on national playing field. Our past design documentation can be found in TECHNICAL section.

EuRoC
CHALLENGE WITHOUT LIMITS

With the assistance of the ITIM and Ion Ursu physics research laboratories we fully intend to participate in the 2025 edition of the European Rocketry Competition, awaiting only preselection standard. New infrastructure, members, and backing gives us the unbridled confidence of solid performance - cementing the foundations of a future unexplored industry in Romania. Our team is ready, Portugal. Are you?

NGO
A FIRST STEP TO SPACE

An NGO - also known as a Non-Governmental Organization - is the first of many planned steps we intend to take to guarantee a foothold in the aerospace sector. If all goes well, we would be able to not only access sponsor backing, but also EU backed grants through NextGenerationEU and Romanian backed grants through the START-UP NATION and Electric Up programs. Not the first to try, but perhaps the first to succeed.
